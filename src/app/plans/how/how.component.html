Why we created Lazy Menu?

Why?

We are an average couple who lives and works in London. Like all, we try to pay attention to how we eat.

However, urban life leaves us very little or no time to cook. Not to mention grocery shopping…

Our routine was to get groceries over the weekend. Buy random ingredients and leave figuring out dishes to cook till later.

Commuting home every night, we would be puzzling out “What to cook for dinner?” or “What’s in our fridge?“. Tired to think, I would pop in the nearest takeaway and get whatever the shop has to offer.
Than, by the end of the week, we had to bin all the rotten vegetables…
Sad... but true!

What?

With that in mind, we created a vast list of, hassle-free and nutritious, dishes we like. This way we can rotate and methodize recipes for every week

Plus weekly shopping list download for the generated menu.

How?

Since we both happen to be Web Developers, having some spare time, we've made it the way we know the best. For our own use and anybody else facing the same cooking dilemma.
All are welcome to use it free
